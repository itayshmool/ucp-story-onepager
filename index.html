01 / Context

WHAT
IS UCP?

The Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) is an open-source standard developed by Google to enable seamless "agentic commerce" — allowing AI agents to directly search for products, check inventory, and complete purchases on behalf of users. It acts as a universal language for AI, reducing checkout friction and eliminating the need for custom integrations between merchants and various AI platforms.
